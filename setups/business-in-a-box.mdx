## What's underneath

Business-in-a-box is built on Brain Map, so it inherits the same canvas, the same marketplace, and the same approval-first safety model. On top of that you get the customer-facing layer: the site visitors see, the CRM that tracks them, and the agent team that runs intake to follow-up.

It started as a solar-company demo and reskins for any service business: home services, trades, clinics, agencies.

## Who it's for

Best for service businesses that want the whole thing running together, not just an assistant. If you are not there yet, [Brain Map](/setups/brain-map) gives you the canvas and tools without the full front end.
