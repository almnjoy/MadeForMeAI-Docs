**Last updated: May 2026**

MindMap is built on a simple principle: your data is yours. Here is exactly what that means.

## What we collect

**Account information** is the email address and identity your administrator provisions through Authentik single sign-on. We use it to manage access and contact you about your tenant.

**Usage metadata** is basic technical logs (server uptime, error rates, response times). It does not include the content of your conversations.

## What we do NOT collect

- The content of your conversations with your AI
- Files you upload into your workspace
- Data from connected apps (Gmail, Google Drive, and the like)
- Anything that would let us identify what you said or asked

Your AI's memory and conversations stay in your tenant. We do not read them, and we do not use them to train AI models.

## Your data stays in your tenant

MindMap runs each tenant in its own dedicated environment. Your conversations, memory, and files stay within that environment. They are not shared with other tenants, not processed by us, and not used for any purpose outside of running your workspace.

## Connectors and approvals

You enable connectors yourself in the Admin marketplace, across four buckets: Apps, Tools, Skills, and Secrets. MindMap reads from a connected app only when you ask it to. Any action that writes back to a connected app queues for your approval before it runs. Credentials you add as Secrets stay in your tenant.

## Third-party AI providers

Your AI uses language models from third-party providers (Anthropic, OpenAI, or OpenRouter, depending on how your tenant is configured). When you send a message, that provider's API processes it. Each provider has its own privacy policy governing how it handles API inputs. We recommend reviewing their policies if this matters to you.

## Data deletion

If your tenant is closed, your data is held for 30 days and then permanently deleted, including all conversations, memory, and files. To have it deleted sooner, email support@madeformeai.com.
