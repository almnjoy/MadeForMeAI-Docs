## How it works

Three steps. Under 2 minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pick a name for your AI">
    Go to [madeformeai.com/early-access](https://madeformeai.com/early-access) and choose a name. Your AI will live at `yourname.madeformeai.com`. That's your space, nobody else's.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your plan and check out">
    BYOK, Standard, or Power. No commitment. Cancel anytime. Most people start with Standard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your email and log in">
    You'll get a welcome email with your login token within a couple minutes. Paste it at your AI's URL and you're in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Just start talking">
    No tutorial. No setup wizard. No learning curve. Just say what you need and your AI handles it.
  </Step>
</Steps>
