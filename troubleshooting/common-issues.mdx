---
title: "Common Issues"
description: "Quick fixes for the problems people hit most often in MindMap, from login to the approval gate."
---

Most questions about MindMap fall into a handful of buckets. Start here, then follow the link for the full walkthrough if you need it.

<AccordionGroup>

  <Accordion title="I can't log in">
    MindMap signs you in through Authentik (single sign-on) at your team's URL. You don't set a separate MindMap password.

    If you keep landing back on the sign-in page, your session may have expired, or your account may not be in the group that has access yet. Sign out fully, close the tab, and sign in again. If that still bounces you, ask your admin to confirm your account is in the right group.

    [Full login troubleshooting](/troubleshooting/cant-log-in)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="MindMap paused on a 'tool call' and stopped typing">
    That pause is normal. When MindMap reads a calendar, searches the web, or opens a file, it shows a tool-call chip and works in the background for a few seconds. Reads run on their own and come straight back with the result.

    Give it 10 to 30 seconds. If it sits far longer than that, see [Slow or Stuck](/troubleshooting/slow-or-stuck).

    [What tool calls are](/understanding/tool-calls)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="MindMap said it queued something and is 'waiting for approval'">
    Also normal, and it's the point of the product. Every read happens instantly. Every write (sending an email, creating a record, posting to a connected app) gets queued instead of run, so nothing leaves your workspace until you say yes.

    Open the approvals panel, review the proposed action, and click **Approve** or **Reject**. Once you approve it, MindMap carries it out and reports back.

    <Note>If you trust a specific action enough to skip the prompt, an admin can add a trust rule for it in the marketplace. Until then, writes always wait for you.</Note>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="A feature or app isn't showing up">
    MindMap ships with features turned off until your admin enables them. Apps, Tools, Skills, and Secrets all live in the Admin marketplace, split into those four buckets.

    If a connector or page you expected is missing, it's probably switched off. Ask your admin to open the marketplace and enable it for your tenant.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="A connector says it 'needs a key' or won't run">
    Connectors that talk to outside services (Gmail, a CRM, GitHub, and the like) need a credential saved in the **Secrets** bucket of the marketplace. If the key is missing, expired, or was rotated on the other side, the connector shows as needing setup and its tools won't run.

    An admin can add or update the key under Admin > Marketplace > Secrets. After saving it, the connector's tools become available on your next message.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="MindMap can't see my computer's files">
    MindMap runs in the cloud, not on your machine, so it can't reach local files on its own. Upload a file straight into the chat with the attachment button, or connect a cloud source (like Google Drive) from the marketplace and point MindMap at it there.

    [Why cloud, not local](/understanding/cloud-not-local)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="MindMap acts like it forgot who I am">
    Memory in MindMap is shared across your whole tenant, but it isn't tied to a single chat. If you cleared a chat or moved to a different surface or Space, the conversation history looks empty even though saved memories are intact.

    To store something durable, tell MindMap plainly: "Remember that..." and it saves the fact for next time.

    [More on memory](/troubleshooting/my-ai-forgot-me)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="MindMap is slow or unresponsive">
    First check whether it's mid tool call or waiting on your approval (both look like a stall but aren't). A long web search or a slow connector call can also take a while.

    If it's genuinely frozen, refresh the page and resend. The full checklist, including how to stop a run, is here:

    [Slow or Stuck](/troubleshooting/slow-or-stuck)
  </Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>

Still stuck? Use the in-app feedback button, or reach your admin, who can check the marketplace, secrets, and group access for your tenant.
