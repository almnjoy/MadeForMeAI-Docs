---
title: "Can't Log In"
description: "Fix sign-in problems in MindMap, including sign-in loops, group access, expired sessions, and the wrong workspace URL."
---

MindMap signs you in through your team's single sign-on (SSO). There's no separate MindMap password and no email login link. If you can't get in, work through these in order.

<Steps>

  <Step title="Confirm you're at the right workspace URL">
    MindMap lives at your team's own address (for example `your-tenant.example.com`). If you're on a different workspace URL or a generic landing page, sign-in won't recognize you.

    Check the address bar against the link your admin gave you. If you're not sure of the exact URL, ask your admin for it directly.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Sign out fully, then sign back in">
    A stale session is the most common cause of a sign-in loop. Click your account menu and sign out, close the tab, then open the workspace URL fresh. You'll be prompted to sign in again.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Try a private window">
    Old cookies can trap you on the sign-in page even after you authenticate. Open an incognito or private window and sign in there. If that works, clear cookies for your workspace domain in your normal browser and try again.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Ask your admin to check your access group">
    Access to MindMap is granted by your access group (managed by your admin). If you authenticate but still bounce back to the sign-in screen, or you see an "access denied" message, your account probably isn't in the group that has access yet.

    Ask your admin to add you to the right access group. This is the fix for "it logs me in but won't let me in."
  </Step>

</Steps>

<Warning>
If you authenticate, get into MindMap, and then get kicked back to sign-in after a while, your session expired. That's expected behavior. Sign in again and you'll pick up where you left off. Saved data and memory are not affected by a session expiring.
</Warning>

## What's going on, by symptom

| What you see | Likely cause | What to do |
|---|---|---|
| You keep landing on the sign-in page | Stale session or old cookies | Sign out fully, then use a private window |
| "Access denied" or you loop after authenticating | Your account isn't in an allowed group | Ask your admin to add you to the right access group |
| You were in, now it sends you back to sign-in | Session expired | Sign in again, nothing is lost |
| The page doesn't recognize you at all | Wrong workspace URL | Confirm the address with your admin |

## Still locked out

If none of the above gets you in, send your admin two details: the exact URL you're using and what happens when you try (a loop, an "access denied" screen, a blank page). Those two facts tell them whether to fix your access group or the workspace address.
