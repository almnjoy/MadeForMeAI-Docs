## What makes it different

**It's set up for you.** No command line, no config files, no temporary passwords pasted between tabs. I handle the plumbing.

**It's built around you.** I look at your actual day, find the friction, and shape the setup to fit it instead of the other way around.

**It remembers you across every conversation.** Tell it your business idea on Monday and it still knows on Friday. No re-explaining yourself every time you open it.

**There's an expert on call.** A decade in IT support means when something breaks or confuses you, you have someone who will just fix it.
