Save an approved multi-step sequence as a named, reusable play and recall it in one line. A play replays each step through its normal approval gate, so a saved play never bypasses approval.

## What you need

Nothing to supply, it's built in. Plays live in the `workflows` table (per tenant).

## Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable the app">
    Go to the marketplace and enable Saved Plays. No key required.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How it works

`save_workflow` stores a named, ordered list of plain-language steps. `run_workflow` recalls a play and returns its steps so the agent performs them in order this turn. Each underlying write still hits its own approval gate.

To run a play on a schedule, there is no always-on timer in the app. Add an n8n Schedule trigger that POSTs `{"message":"run my Monday-AR play"}` to your canvas API endpoint (`/api/canvas`). That keeps scheduling in your existing automation layer and still routes every write through approval.

## What you can ask

- "Save this as a play called Monday-AR: run ar_review over 30 days, then nudge the 3 oldest" (saves the play)
- "Run my Monday-AR play" (recalls and runs it)
- "What plays do I have?" (lists your plays)

## Limitations

- Steps are instructions the agent executes, not a hard-coded macro engine. Good for repeatable routines, not for unattended bulk automation.
- No branching or conditionals yet (keep plays linear).

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can a play run unattended overnight?">
    It can be triggered on a schedule, but any write in it still queues an approval, so it will wait for your tap unless the steps are all reads.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
