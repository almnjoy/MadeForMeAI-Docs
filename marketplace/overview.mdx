Brain Map ships with a marketplace of apps and plugins. Each one is a tool the agent can use on your behalf: read your network, pull your revenue, send a Telegram, draft a review request, drive a browser. You turn on the ones you want from the Admin marketplace, add any keys they need, and they light up.

Two rules hold across everything here:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Reads run instantly" icon="bolt">
    Looking something up, checking status, pulling a balance: these happen live, no approval needed.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Writes are approval-gated" icon="shield-check">
    Sending, creating, deleting, or moving anything queues for your approval first. Nothing fires on its own unless you set a Trust Rule.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
