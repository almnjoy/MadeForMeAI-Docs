Live revenue and bank balances in MindMap. Two independent sources under one toggle:

- **Stripe**: your available and pending revenue.
- **Plaid**: bank, credit, and investment balances across your linked institutions, plus a
  net worth number.

It's **read-only**. Plaid here never moves money, it only reads balances. You get the agent
tool (`get_money`), the **Money page**, and the **Connect a bank** flow, all from the one
Money card.

**Plaid is managed for you.** It runs on the operator's Plaid keys, so there are no Plaid keys
to enter or edit. You just link your bank through the Connect-a-bank flow. **Stripe** is the
one side you configure yourself, with your own Stripe key.

## HOW TO SETUP

1. Open MindMap, go to **Admin** (the marketplace).
2. Click the **Money** card.
3. For revenue: paste your **Stripe secret key** into its write-only box, hit **Save**.
4. Flip **Enable** on.
5. For balances, open the **Money** page and click **Connect a bank** to link each account.
   Plaid is already provided, so there's nothing to set up first, just link your bank.

It applies on your next message, no restart.

## WHAT USER NEEDS TO GET

**Stripe (revenue), one key:**

- A **Stripe secret key**. Dashboard → Developers → API keys. Use a **restricted key** with
  read access only (Balance read, Charges read, Payouts read) so the key can never move money.
  That's all Stripe needs.

**Plaid (balances), nothing to get, just link a bank:**

- **No keys.** Plaid runs on the operator's keys, managed for you. You don't enter or edit
  anything.
- Just a **linked bank**: on the Money page, **Connect a bank** opens Plaid Link, you log into
  your bank, and Plaid hands MindMap a token it stores in its own database. Repeat per
  institution to add more.

## HOW IT WORKS

- MindMap calls the **Stripe** API with your key and the **Plaid** API with the operator's
  managed keys. Your Stripe key stays in your pod, never in the browser or the repo, and the
  key box is write-only.
- **Stripe** returns your balance (available + pending), recent charges, and recent payouts.
- **Plaid** uses `/accounts/get` (cached balances), so it works without the realtime Balance
  product. It tallies **cash** (depository), **credit**, and **investment**, and computes
  **net worth = cash + investment − credit**. Each linked bank is one stored token; balances
  are read across all of them.
- **Connect a bank** runs the standard Plaid Link flow. The exchanged access token is saved in
  MindMap's own database, separate from any payment portal.
- The **Money page** is the visual version: balances, accounts, and the Connect-a-bank button.
  The agent's `get_money` drops the same numbers as a tile on the canvas.
- Keys are read **live** (the marketplace key box applies on the next request, no restart).

## WHAT CAN BE CALLED VIA AI

- "what's my cash position" / "how much cash do I have": Plaid balances
- "what's my net worth": cash + investments − credit across linked banks
- "how much revenue is available in Stripe" / "what's pending in Stripe": Stripe balance
- "show me the money tile": drops the Money tile on the canvas

Whichever side is configured is what comes back. If only Stripe is set, you get revenue; if
only Plaid is set, you get balances; both set, you get both.

## LIMITATIONS

- **Read-only.** Nothing here moves money, sends a payout, or initiates a transfer. Plaid is
  balances only. Use a read-only restricted Stripe key to keep it that way at the source.
- **You still have to link a bank.** Plaid is provided, but it has nothing to read until you
  run **Connect a bank** on the Money page. No link, no balances.
- **Balances are cached** (`/accounts/get`), not realtime to the second. Fine for net worth and
  cash position, not for intra-minute trading numbers.
- **Transactions** are available per account (`/api/money/plaid/transactions`) but only if the
  Transactions product is enabled on that Plaid item; otherwise it returns empty.

## FAQ

**Can it move my money?** No. It reads balances and revenue only. Plaid never moves money here,
and a read-only Stripe key can't either.

**Do I need both Stripe and Plaid?** No. They're independent. Either one lights the feature up.
Stripe is for revenue, Plaid is for bank/credit/investment balances.

**Why doesn't Plaid show anything?** Plaid is provided, but you still have to **Connect a bank**
on the Money page so there's an account to read. No linked bank, no balances.

**Do I set up Plaid keys?** No. Plaid is managed for you on the operator's keys. You only link
your bank. The one key you provide is your **Stripe** key, for revenue.

**Is my Stripe key safe?** The key box is write-only. Your key is saved to your pod, never shown
back to the browser, never in the repo.

**Can I limit which banks show?** Yes, but it's an operator setting (`PLAID_INSTITUTION_ALLOW`),
not something you edit. By default everything you've linked shows.
