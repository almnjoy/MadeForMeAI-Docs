Live revenue and bank balances in MindMap. Two independent sources under one toggle:

- **Stripe**: your available and pending revenue.
- **Plaid**: bank, credit, and investment balances across your linked institutions, plus a
  net worth number.

It's **read-only**. Plaid here never moves money, it only reads balances. You get the agent
tool (`get_money`), the **Money page**, and the **Connect a bank** flow, all from the one
Money card. Stripe and Plaid are independent: turn on one, the other, or both. Either one on
its own lights the feature up.

## HOW TO SETUP

1. Open MindMap, go to **Admin** (the marketplace).
2. Click the **Money** card.
3. Add whichever side you want:
   - **Stripe:** paste your **Stripe secret key** into its write-only box, hit **Save**.
   - **Plaid:** paste your **Plaid client ID** and **Plaid secret**, hit **Save** on each.
4. Flip **Enable** on.
5. For Plaid balances, open the **Money** page and click **Connect a bank** to link each
   account (this is the per-user bank login step, see below).

It applies on your next message, no restart. The card shows **connected** as soon as Stripe is
set, or as soon as both Plaid keys are set.

## WHAT USER NEEDS TO GET

**Stripe (revenue), one key:**

- A **Stripe secret key**. Dashboard → Developers → API keys. Use a **restricted key** with
  read access only (Balance read, Charges read, Payouts read) so the key can never move money.
  That's all Stripe needs.

**Plaid (balances), your app keys, then each user links a bank:**

- **Plaid client ID + secret** from your Plaid dashboard. This is the **operator's** app
  credential and needs **production approval** for real banks (sandbox keys only hit Plaid's
  fake banks). One set of app keys serves everyone on the box.
- Then, per person, a **linked bank**: on the Money page, **Connect a bank** opens Plaid Link,
  you log into your bank, and Plaid hands MindMap a token it stores in its own database. The
  bank login is the **link step**, not a replacement for the app keys. No app keys, no Link.

Optional Plaid env knobs (defaults are fine):

- `PLAID_ENV`: `production` by default. Set `sandbox` to test with fake banks.
- `PLAID_INSTITUTION_ALLOW`: comma-separated allowlist; only show institutions whose name/id
  matches. Leave blank to show all linked accounts.
- `PLAID_LINK_PRODUCTS`: `transactions` by default (gives balances + transactions).
- `PLAID_REDIRECT_URI`: your registered OAuth redirect, needed for OAuth banks.

## HOW IT WORKS

- MindMap calls the **Stripe** and **Plaid** APIs directly with your keys. Keys stay in your
  pod, never in the browser or the repo, and the key boxes are write-only.
- **Stripe** returns your balance (available + pending), recent charges, and recent payouts.
- **Plaid** uses `/accounts/get` (cached balances), so it works without the realtime Balance
  product. It tallies **cash** (depository), **credit**, and **investment**, and computes
  **net worth = cash + investment − credit**. Each linked bank is one stored token; balances
  are read across all of them.
- **Connect a bank** runs the standard Plaid Link flow. The exchanged access token is saved in
  MindMap's own database, separate from any payment portal.
- The **Money page** is the visual version: balances, accounts, and the Connect-a-bank button.
  The agent's `get_money` drops the same numbers as a tile on the canvas.
- Keys are read **live** (the marketplace key box applies on the next request, no restart).

## WHAT CAN BE CALLED VIA AI

- "what's my cash position" / "how much cash do I have": Plaid balances
- "what's my net worth": cash + investments − credit across linked banks
- "how much revenue is available in Stripe" / "what's pending in Stripe": Stripe balance
- "show me the money tile": drops the Money tile on the canvas

Whichever side is configured is what comes back. If only Stripe is set, you get revenue; if
only Plaid is set, you get balances; both set, you get both.

## LIMITATIONS

- **Read-only.** Nothing here moves money, sends a payout, or initiates a transfer. Plaid is
  balances only. Use a read-only restricted Stripe key to keep it that way at the source.
- **Plaid needs production approval** for real banks. Sandbox keys only reach Plaid's test
  institutions. Until your Plaid app is production-approved, real balances won't link.
- **Each person links their own bank.** The app keys make Plaid available; they don't link an
  account on their own. No "Connect a bank" step, no balances.
- **Balances are cached** (`/accounts/get`), not realtime to the second. Fine for net worth and
  cash position, not for intra-minute trading numbers.
- **Transactions** are available per account (`/api/money/plaid/transactions`) but only if the
  Transactions product is enabled on that Plaid item; otherwise it returns empty.

## FAQ

**Can it move my money?** No. It reads balances and revenue only. Plaid never moves money here,
and a read-only Stripe key can't either.

**Do I need both Stripe and Plaid?** No. They're independent. Either one lights the feature up.
Stripe is for revenue, Plaid is for bank/credit/investment balances.

**Why doesn't Plaid show anything after I added the keys?** The keys make Plaid available, but
you still have to **Connect a bank** on the Money page so there's an account to read. Also check
`PLAID_ENV` is `production` (sandbox only sees fake banks).

**Whose Plaid keys are these?** The operator's app keys (yours). One set serves everyone on the
box. Each user then links their own bank on top of that.

**Are my keys safe?** The key boxes are write-only. Keys are saved to your pod, never shown back
to the browser, never in the repo.

**Can I limit which banks show?** Yes. Set `PLAID_INSTITUTION_ALLOW` to a comma-separated list
of institution names or ids; only matches show. Blank shows everything you've linked.
