# Review Requests

After a paid job, shoot, or milestone, draft an approval-gated ask for a Google or Yelp review with
your review link. Tracks who was asked.

<Note>
This is a lightweight draft-and-track helper, not a polished send-it-for-you feature. The reliable
way to actually deliver and time review asks is to wire it through your automation connectors (n8n,
Zapier, or Composio). Use the agent to draft and queue the ask, then let a connector send it.
</Note>

## HOW TO SETUP
Marketplace -> Review Requests. Optionally set `REVIEW_URL` (your Google/Yelp review link) so asks
prefill it. No key required.

## WHAT USER NEEDS TO GET
Your public review link (optional but recommended).

## HOW IT WORKS
`request_review` drafts the message and queues an approval. On approve, the request is marked sent
and returns the message + link. The agent does not send it for you. Hand the result to a connector
(n8n, Zapier, or Composio) or your Telegram channel to deliver the SMS or email. Stored in the
`review_requests` table.

## WHAT CAN BE CALLED VIA AI
- "Ask Acme for a review, text 303..." -> request_review (approval-gated)
- "Who have I asked for reviews?" -> list_review_requests

## LIMITATIONS
- This is not a heavily tested, first-class feature. It drafts and tracks only. Treat delivery as a
  connector job.
- Sending the SMS or email routes through your channel. There is no direct sender wired unless you
  use Telegram, n8n, Zapier, or Composio.
- No automatic "after paid invoice" trigger. Pair with the AR watcher or a saved play plus an n8n or
  Zapier schedule to handle the timing.

## FAQ
Q: Can it auto-send after a job? A: Draft + queue yes; automatic timing via an n8n schedule that
asks the agent to run the review play.
