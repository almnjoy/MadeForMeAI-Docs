How you sign in depends on how MindMap is hosted for you:

- **Cloud hosted:** you'll be provided a secure login via email. Sign-in is handled for you behind the scenes, so there is no separate MindMap password to set up.
- **Self hosted (coming soon):** bring your own authentication, enabled in configuration.

## Sign in

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your workspace URL">
    Go to the address you were given. It is a single web address for your workspace, like `your-team.example.com`. Bookmark it so you can get back fast.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in">
    Enter the secure login provided for your workspace. If your setup uses a passkey, a code, or another second factor, you'll be prompted for it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Land on Chat">
    Once you're signed in, you land on the Chat surface, ready to go.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Staying signed in

Once you sign in on a device, your browser keeps the session. You will not sign in again on that device until the session expires or you sign out. Your conversations, memory, and files live in the cloud, so a new browser or a new device shows the same workspace after you sign in.

<Note>On a shared or public computer, sign out when you finish.</Note>

## Sign out

Use the **Sign out** button in the header. It clears your MindMap session and signs you out cleanly.

## Stuck on the sign-in screen?

If you sign in and the page bounces you straight back to the login, work through these:

- **Clear the loop.** Close the tab, open a fresh one, and go to your workspace URL again. A stale half-finished session sometimes causes the bounce.
- **Allow cookies.** MindMap needs cookies to hold your session. Private or incognito windows and strict cookie blockers can break it. Try a normal window.
- **Check the address.** Confirm you are using the exact workspace URL you were given, not an old bookmark.
- **Confirm your access.** If the login is rejected or says you have no access, your account may not be added to MindMap yet. Ask your administrator to grant you access.
