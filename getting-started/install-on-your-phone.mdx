---
title: "Install on Your Phone"
description: "Add MindMap to your phone's home screen so it opens full screen like a regular app."
---

MindMap runs in any modern phone browser. No app store, no download. Add it to your home screen and it opens full screen, like a native app, on the same account and data you use on a computer.

{/* MEDIA gif: on an iPhone, tap Safari's Share button, scroll to Add to Home Screen, tap Add, then open the new icon | caption: "iOS: Share, then Add to Home Screen." */}

## Open MindMap on your phone

Open your tenant URL in your phone's browser and sign in through Authentik. On a phone the layout adapts for you: the sidebar folds into a menu, pages go full width, and you land on **Chat**, the surface that fits a small screen best. Tap any artifact to open it full screen, then close it to go back to the conversation.

## Add to your home screen

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="iPhone or iPad (Safari)">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open MindMap in Safari">
        Use Safari for this. Chrome on iPhone cannot install it.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Tap Share">
        Tap the **Share** button, the box with an arrow pointing up.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Tap Add to Home Screen">
        Scroll the share menu until you find **Add to Home Screen**, then tap it.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Tap Add">
        Keep or change the name, then tap **Add** in the top corner.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Android (Chrome)">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open MindMap in Chrome">
        Go to your tenant URL in Chrome.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Open the menu">
        Tap the **menu** (the three dots) in the top corner. Some phones show an install banner at the bottom instead, which works too.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Tap Install app">
        Tap **Install app**. Some phones label it **Add to Home screen**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Confirm">
        Tap **Add** or **Install** to confirm.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

The icon and name match your workspace's branding.

## It opens like an app

Tap the new icon and MindMap launches full screen, with no browser bars. The installed app is the live site, so it updates on its own. You always get the current version with no manual update step. It is a home-screen app, not a native one, so you will not find it in the App Store or Google Play. That is by design.

<Note>You can keep using MindMap straight in the browser if you prefer. Installing just gives you the one-tap, full-screen version.</Note>

## Canvas is desktop-first

On a phone, Chat and the standard pages are the good experience. The visual **Canvas** board is built for a larger screen, so use it on a tablet or computer.

<Warning>There is no offline mode. MindMap needs an internet connection to work.</Warning>
